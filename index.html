Our Story

What started as two gamers crossing paths online slowly grew into something neither of us expected. Between late-night MapleStory sessions and long conversations that stretched past midnight, we found a connection that felt effortless and real.

From our first awkward meetup to exploring hawker centres together, adopting way too many house plants, and navigating the chaos of BTO applications, every chapter has been better with the other person in it. We're excited to start the next one with all of you there.
